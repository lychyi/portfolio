DesignStudio is a line of software that creates custom assay designs for Illumina's two major category of products: the microarray and next-generation sequencing. It has been one of the products where I was in charge of both the user experience design and front end engineering. The value of having a solid design language and a well-made developer experience proved pivotal in meeting timelines despite changes.

Develop the developer experience

Webpack, Browsersync, npm scripts, VS Code, run tasks.

Establish a design language

Respect the brand and look for opportunities to innovate. Zero states. Voice and tone. D3 for charts. Helix loader.

Focus on performance early

Use lightweight imagery, pngs, svgs.

Seek feedback early and often

Build a relationship with technical support, internal users, and marketing.
